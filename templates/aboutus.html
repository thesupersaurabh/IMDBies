About Us

Welcome to our movie and series search platform! We've created this site using VidScript embedded for streaming content and IMDbPy to scrape movie details.

Tip: uBlock Origin for Ad-Free Streaming

For an even better experience, we recommend using uBlock Origin to block ads and enjoy uninterrupted streaming. Install it on your browser, and if you're on Android, you can use Kiwi Browser with uBlock Origin for ad-free mobile streaming.

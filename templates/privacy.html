Introduction

Welcome to IMDBies's Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our streaming service.

When you use IMDBies, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including your browsing patterns, pages visited, time spent on pages, and search queries.

  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.
  • Watchlist Information: If you save items to your watchlist, we store this information locally on your device.
